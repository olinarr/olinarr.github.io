I am a PhD candidate under the supervision of Stefan Woltran at the Database and Artificial Intelligence Group (DBAI) at TU Wien. Here, I am part of the LogiCS-COFUND doctoral programme.

My main research areas are artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems and automated reasoning, and I am particularly interested in computational social choice.

Previously, I obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at the University of Verona and a Master's Degree in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Amsterdam. There, I wrote my Master's thesis under the supervision of Arthur Boixel and Ulle Endriss, which won the university-wide UvA Thesis Prize 2022. After completing my Master's, I stayed in Amsterdam as a research assistant at the COMSOC Group at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC). If you are interested, have a look at my detailed curriculum vitae.

Besides theorems and algorithms, I am also into cinema and poetry, and play the classical guitar.
